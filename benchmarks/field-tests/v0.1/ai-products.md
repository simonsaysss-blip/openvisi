## Readout

AI-native brands are culturally visible, but that does not guarantee a rich
crawl-visible source layer. This contrast is useful for explaining why OpenVisi
separates brand awareness from AI-readable Structure.

The low scores should not be interpreted as low real-world AI Visibility. They
show that the lightweight crawler saw limited explicit homepage source
structure.

## Article Angle

AI companies are not automatically AI-readable.
