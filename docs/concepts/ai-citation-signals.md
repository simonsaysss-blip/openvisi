# AI Citation Signals

One-line definition: AI Citation Signals are public signals that increase the
likelihood of being cited in AI-generated answers.

## Why It Matters

AI Citation Signals are not backlinks, but they may become one of the core trust
layers for LLM-powered discovery.

## How OpenVisi Measures It

OpenVisi evaluates official sources, citation-ready pages, external references,
structured data, freshness signals, and whether claims are supported by stable
URLs.

## How Teams Can Improve It

Create source pages for important claims, keep docs fresh, publish structured
FAQs, make official URLs stable, and ensure third-party references point to the
right entity.
