# AI Visibility

One-line definition: AI Visibility is the measurable presence, accuracy,
citation quality, and competitive position of an entity across AI-generated
answers.

## Why It Matters

AI-generated answers increasingly shape how people discover products, companies,
documentation, services, and open-source projects. OpenVisi treats AI Visibility
as a measurable system, not a vague marketing idea.

## How OpenVisi Measures It

OpenVisi measures AI Visibility through four dimensions:

- Presence: whether AI mentions the entity.
- Accuracy: whether AI describes the entity correctly.
- Citation: which sources AI uses to understand or support the answer.
- Competition: whether AI routes the user toward competitors.

## How Teams Can Improve It

Teams can improve AI Visibility by clarifying entity definitions, publishing
stable documentation, adding structured metadata, maintaining official source
freshness, and making comparisons and category pages easier to parse.
