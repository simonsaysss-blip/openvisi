# AI Visibility Score

One-line definition: AI Visibility Score is a composite score that summarizes
the measurable AI Visibility signals for a target entity.

## Why It Matters

A single score helps teams track a snapshot and compare repeat scans, but it
should never hide the evidence behind the number.

## How OpenVisi Measures It

OpenVisi v0.1 treats the score as a transparent composite derived from:

- `answerPresence`
- `answerShare`
- `entityClarity`
- `citationCoverage`
- `competitorDisplacement`
- `machineReadableTrust`
- `aiCitationSignals`

The score must remain explainable and should not become a black box.

## How Teams Can Improve It

Improve the component metrics first: clarify the entity, strengthen official
sources, increase citation-ready public evidence, and reduce competitor
confusion in category and comparison contexts.
