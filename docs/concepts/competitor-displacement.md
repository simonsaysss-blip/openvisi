# Competitor Displacement

One-line definition: Competitor Displacement occurs when AI answers recommend,
cite, or describe competitors instead of the target brand.

## Why It Matters

Commercial discovery depends on where the answer routes attention. A brand may
have strong documentation and still lose demand if AI answers consistently name
alternatives for category, problem-solution, or comparison prompts.

## How OpenVisi Measures It

OpenVisi treats displacement as a competitive layer metric across prompt packs:
how often competitors are named, recommended, cited, or given stronger answer
share than the target entity.

## How Teams Can Improve It

Clarify positioning, publish comparison pages, explain ideal customer fit, make
category language explicit, and close source gaps that cause AI systems to rely
on competitor-controlled narratives.

## Related OpenVisi Metrics

`competitorDisplacement`, `categoryShare`,
`alternativeRecommendationRate`, `comparisonVisibility`, `answerShare`.
