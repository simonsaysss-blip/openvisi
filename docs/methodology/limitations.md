# OpenVisi Limitations

OpenVisi is methodology-first and should be explicit about what its metrics can
and cannot prove.

## Known Limitations

- AI answers are probabilistic.
- Outputs can vary by model and time.
- Citations are surface-dependent.
- AI Visibility is not identical to SEO ranking.
- Metrics should be interpreted directionally in early versions.

## Practical Implications

A visibility report is a snapshot, not a permanent truth. It should record the
prompt pack, model or surface, date, source evidence, and methodology version
when those fields are available.

## Non-Goals

OpenVisi does not promise answer inclusion, citations, recommendations, or
commercial outcomes. It defines vocabulary, schemas, diagnostics, and
methodology for measuring AI Visibility with transparent evidence.
