# OpenVisi Project Brief

## North Star

OpenVisi defines an open-source measurement layer and canonical vocabulary for AI Visibility in the LLM search era.

## Canonical Definition

AI Visibility is the measurable presence, accuracy, citation quality, and competitive position of an entity across AI-generated answers.

## What OpenVisi Is

- Open-source AI Visibility measurement infrastructure
- Canonical vocabulary for AI Visibility diagnostics
- Schema-backed benchmark and report surface
- Artifact-first benchmark pipeline
- Reproducible Visibility Reports

## What OpenVisi Is Not

- Not an AI SEO tool
- Not SEO for ChatGPT
- Not a rank-higher-in-AI product
- Not a growth hack
- Not a prompt optimization tool
- Not a SaaS dashboard-first product
- Not a ranking or citation guarantee system

## Current RC Truth

- The CLI artifact pipeline is the current RC truth.
- The web surface is a schema-backed directional demo.
- Demo scores are directional samples, not provider-verified final benchmark results.
- Legacy scan is maintained for compatibility and does not produce a final AI Visibility Score.

## Measurement Model

- Presence
- Accuracy
- Citation
- Competitive
