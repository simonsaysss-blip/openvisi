# What Is AI Visibility?

AI Visibility is the measurable presence, accuracy, citation quality, and
competitive position of an entity across AI-generated answers.

This definition matters because AI-generated answers are not just another list
of links. They summarize categories, recommend alternatives, cite sources, and
sometimes omit relevant entities entirely.

OpenVisi defines AI Visibility as a measurement layer with four dimensions:
presence, accuracy, citation, and competition.

## Answer Presence

Answer Presence asks a basic question: does the target entity appear in the
answer at all?

If a company, product, or project is absent from relevant category prompts, it
cannot earn citation coverage or answer share in that surface.

## AI Visibility Score

AI Visibility Score is a composite score. It should summarize visibility, but it
must remain explainable through component metrics such as Answer Presence,
Entity Clarity, Citation Coverage, and Competitor Displacement.

The score should never become a black box.

## Citation Coverage

Citation Coverage measures whether AI-generated answers cite or rely on sources
connected to the target entity.

This is different from simply being mentioned. A cited official source can
indicate that the answer has an anchor for the entity's facts.

## Competitor Displacement

Competitor Displacement happens when AI answers recommend, cite, or describe
competitors instead of the target entity.

That matters commercially because AI-generated answers can shape discovery,
comparison, and shortlist behavior.

## Why OpenVisi Exists

OpenVisi exists to make AI Visibility measurable, explainable, and reproducible.
It provides vocabulary, methodology, schemas, and report surfaces that can be
audited and improved in public.
