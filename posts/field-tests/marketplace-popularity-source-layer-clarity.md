# Marketplace Popularity Is Not Source-layer Clarity

OpenVisi ran a small crawl-diagnostic scan across Shopify, Amazon, and Etsy on
May 26, 2026.

Commerce platforms are useful for AI Visibility testing because they separate
brand awareness, marketplace scale, seller documentation, product data, and
public source clarity.

## The Signal

| Domain      | AI Visibility Score | Entity Clarity | Citation Coverage Proxy |
| ----------- | ------------------- | -------------- | ----------------------- |
| shopify.com | 66                  | 0.85           | 0.75                    |
| etsy.com    | 46                  | 0.63           | 0.53                    |
| amazon.com  | 42                  | 0.45           | 0.66                    |

The pattern is simple: marketplace popularity and AI-readable source structure
are different measurement surfaces.

## Why This Matters

AI-generated answers often compare commerce platforms by seller fit, audience,
pricing, product category, fulfillment model, and marketplace trust.

That requires source material that is structured enough to parse and cite.

## Follow-up

Future scans should compare homepages with seller pages, category pages, product
pages, policy pages, and help centers.
